Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 3, 2026

Rexx ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the Rexx app.

Information We Collect

Local Data
Rexx stores your recommendations, categories, and notes locally on your device. This data is yours and stays on your device.

iCloud Sync
If you have iCloud enabled, your data syncs across your devices using your personal iCloud account. We do not have access to this data—it is stored in your private iCloud container.

Contacts (Optional)
With your permission, Rexx can access your contacts to help you quickly enter who recommended something. Contact data is only used locally and is never uploaded to our servers or shared with third parties.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect personal information like your name, email, or phone number
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools
  • We do not share or sell any data to third parties

Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device and in your personal iCloud account (if enabled). We do not operate servers that store your personal data.

Children's Privacy

Rexx does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is rated 4+ and is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
